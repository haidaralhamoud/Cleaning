Cookies Policy

Last updated: 06/10/2025

At Hembla AB, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website, make our services work properly, and provide relevant information to you.

By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, logins, and activity so it can work more efficiently.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Required for the website to function.
    • Keeping you logged in during a session.
    • Remembering your booking details in the cart.
  • Functional Cookies – Help us remember your preferences and settings.
    • Language selection.
    • Saved addresses for bookings.
  • Analytics Cookies – Help us understand how people use our website.
    • Google Analytics to track visits and popular pages.
    • Identifying navigation issues.
  • Marketing Cookies – Used to show you relevant ads.
    • Retargeting ads on Facebook or Google.
    • Tracking which campaigns brought you to our site.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies come from third-party services we use, such as:

  • Google Analytics (website performance tracking)
  • Facebook Pixel (marketing and ads)
  • Payment providers (secure payment processing)

4. How Long Are Cookies Stored?

  • Session cookies – Deleted automatically when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies – Stay until expired or manually deleted.

5. Managing & Disabling Cookies

You can control cookies by:

  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Using our Cookie Settings tool to choose which cookies you allow.

Note: If you block essential cookies, some parts of the website may not work correctly.

6. Consent

When you visit our website for the first time, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent for non-essential cookies (functional, analytics, and marketing).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in technology, law, or our services.
